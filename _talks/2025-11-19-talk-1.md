<div class="lang-block lang-zh" markdown="1">

简单介绍量子计算中的一种资源：魔法态，并讨论直接蒸馏与通过码切换制备魔法态的方法。最后分享关于中性原子量子计算控制系统的调研情况。

<div class="lang-block lang-en" markdown="1">

This talk introduces magic states as a resource in quantum computation, then discusses direct distillation and magic-state preparation through code-switching. It also includes a short survey of control systems for neutral-atom quantum computing.
