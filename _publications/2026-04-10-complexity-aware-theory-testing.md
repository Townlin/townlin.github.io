This note proposes a complexity-aware theory-testing method derived from Bell witnesses, connecting Bell statistical strength to complexity-based model selection. It provides a theory-testing witness tailored to physicists and outlines a practical toolkit for using Bell witnesses as hypothesis tests within complexity-aware criteria.
