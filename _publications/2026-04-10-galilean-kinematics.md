<div class="lang-block lang-zh" markdown="1">

本文给出可观测量与生成元对偶关系的连续变量版本，说明光滑参考态族如何生成伽利略运动学。该框架概述了参数化态族编码动力学和对称性的条件。

</div>

<div class="lang-block lang-en" markdown="1">

We present a continuous-variable version of the observable-generator duality, showing how a smooth family of reference states can generate Galilean kinematics. The framework outlines conditions under which a parameterized family of states can encode dynamics and symmetries.

</div>
